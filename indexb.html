THE WEEKLY MORNIN' RUN

ABOUT THE MORNIN' RUN

The MORNIN' RUN is a free, timed, 5 km event that will take place every Saturday morning in Grand Baie La Croisette (more locations to follow soon!)
Our aim is to assist in creating healthier and happier people by encouraging the wellbeing of our communities!

WHY PARTICIPATE?

  1. It is good for your health
  2. Everyone can participate, whether young or old
  3. You can get involved as a volunteer
