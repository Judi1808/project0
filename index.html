THE WEEKLY MORNIN' RUN



ABOUT THE MORNIN' RUN

The MORNIN' RUN is a free, timed, 5 km event that takes place, every Saturday morning in Mauritius, Grand Baie La Croisette (more locations to follow soon!)
Our aim is to assist in creating healthier and happier people by encouraging the wellbeing of our communities! Anybody who is unable to be present physically, can join our virtual event on Strava. Check out the registration page for more details!

WHY PARTICIPATE?

  1. It is good for your health
  2. Everyone can participate, whether young or old
  3. You can get involved as a volunteer
